About The Department

Physiotherapy Department — Tanzania Police Force

The Physiotherapy Department's mandate is to maintain the physical fitness and operational readiness of police officers and staff. We provide specialized treatment for duty-related injuries, post-surgical rehabilitation, ergonomic assessments, and preventive care to ensure officers are fit for duty.

Mission: To be a center of excellence in occupational physiotherapy for the Tanzania Police Force, delivering evidence-based rehabilitation, injury prevention and fitness-for-duty services.

Scope: Sports and training injuries, post-operative rehab, musculoskeletal care, chronic pain management, fitness assessments and return-to-duty programs.
