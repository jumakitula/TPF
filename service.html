Our Specialties

Clinical Expertise for the Tanzania Police Force

Focused Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation services tailored to the needs of police personnel: injury recovery, occupational health, and readiness for duty.

Sports & Training Injury Rehabilitation

Assessment and individualized rehab for training-related injuries and conditioning to prevent recurrence.

Orthopedic & Post-Surgical Rehabilitation

Structured post-op recovery programs and progressive loading to restore function.

Occupational Health & Ergonomics

Workplace assessments and ergonomic adjustments to reduce duty-related strain and injuries.

Chronic Pain Management

Multimodal approaches to manage chronic musculoskeletal pain among officers.

Fitness-for-Duty Assessments

Objective testing and return-to-duty protocols to ensure readiness for operational tasks.

Manual Therapy & Electrotherapy Modalities

Evidence-based manual techniques and adjunct modalities to enhance recovery.
